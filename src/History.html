Our Journey...

We are a small team of developers that are eager to bring education into the world of interactivity and vibrant colours. We are inspired to assist parents and students teaching their kids as early as 2 years old. With the Internet, it is now easier than ever to get access to fundamentals of learning neccessary for your kids. We began our development about 5 months, in UMT. What was initially a very simple project for our team, we finally made this improved version of the website a reality. This came into success with the help from our lecturers and friends, who throughout the journey has never stopped supporting our ideas. And for that, we would like to say thank you.
